User Guide

In this busy world people tend to forget to tend to the plants in their home which can lead to bad growth and poor plant heath. Monitoring the health of a plant and keeping them healthy has never been easier! With this system you will know that your plants are being kept at their optimal conditions without the stress of "Have I watered them enough?, Have I watered them too much? Is the soil too dry?", etc.

The first page you will encounter is the account registration page. This page is for you to create a personal login to view your plant data.

  • Step 1: Enter a username, this username will define your plants so you view yours and yours alone.
  • Step 2: Enter a password, this should be complex but something you will remember.
  • Step 3: Re-enter your password, this should match the above password. There will be an error if it doesn't.
  • Step 4: Click Register
  • Registration

This page is for you to login to your existing account

  • Step 1: Enter your username, this username is the one you entered on the registration page.
  • Step 2: Enter your password.
  • Step 3: Click "login", there will be an error if you have entered the wrong username or password.
  • Login

This page will alert you to any detections over or under the limits.

  • Object name: This is the plant that the alarm is being triggered for.
  • Alarm description: This is the type of alarm being triggered. (Over-range, under-range, etc.)
  • Time logged: This is the time that the alarm was triggered.
  • Value: This is the value that was taken from the plant that has triggered the alarm.
  • Priority: This is a value between 1 and 5 that will show how critical this alarm is and how much attention it needs. 1 being critical and needs to be looked at immediately and 5 being not urgent.
  • Alarms

This page is to see all the plants you have registered to your account. Each plant can be clicked on to view current soil moisture, humidity and temperature data.

  • Each plant that is registered will be able to be viewed on this page.
  • The plants are able to be viewed individually by clicking on the plant you would like to see more information on.
  • Once in the specific plant you will be able to see all current data on that plant.
  • This page will also enable you to turn the watring pump to automatic or manual as well as start or stop that specific plants system.
  • Plants

This page will let you control your system as a whole

  • Restart: This will restart the whole system.
  • Off/ On: This will enable you to be able to switch the whole system off or on.
  • Auto/ Manual: This switches the whole system between automatic and manual control.
  • Water Valve: This enables the water valves in the system to all be turned on or off.
